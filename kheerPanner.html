Paneer Kheer

Imagine the luxurious creaminess of homemade paneer beautifully infused with fragrant cardamom, saffron, and garnished with a generous sprinkling of chopped nuts. Each spoonful will transport you to a realm of pure bliss, as the flavors mingle and dance on your palate.
Whether you're a fan of traditional Indian desserts or a dessert enthusiast looking for something new, Paneer Kheer is an absolute must-try. It's the perfect fusion of sweet, creamy, and aromatic, offering a symphony of flavors that will make your taste buds sing.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Rice - 1 Cup
  • whole milk - 1 1/4
  • Medjool dates, pitted and coarsely chopped
  • Pinch cinnamon
  • Kosher salt
  • Berries, chopped apple, toasted sliced almonds, and honey, for serving

    • Prepration Steps

    1. In a saucepan, combine rice, milk, dates, cinnamon, and a pinch of kosher salt.
    2. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer, stirring, until thick and creamy, 5 to 7 minutes.
    3. Serve, topped with berries, apple, almonds, and a drizzle of honey.
