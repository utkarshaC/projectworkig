Gluten-free Pancakes

Calling all pancake lovers! Are you ready to experience the ultimate gluten-free breakfast sensation? Look no further than our mouthwatering recipe for gluten-free pancakes that will have you craving stack after stack.

Our recipe is designed to make your taste buds dance with joy while catering to dietary needs. We've carefully selected a blend of gluten-free flours, ensuring that every bite delivers the same fluffy, melt-in-your-mouth goodness you crave. The result? A stack of pancakes that are sure to satisfy even the most discerning pancake connoisseurs.

Ingredients

  • Gluten-free plain flour-125 g
  • milk-250 ml
  • Butter
  • Egg-1

    • Prepration Steps

    1. Put the flour in a bowl and make a well in the centre.
    2. Crack the egg in the middle and pour in a quarter of the milk.
    3. Use an electric or balloon whisk to thoroughly combine the mixture.
    4. Once you have a paste, mix in another quarter and once lump free, mix in the remaining milk.
    5. Leave to rest for 20 mins. Stir again before using.
    6. Serve with agave syrup and a squeeze of orange juice or your pancake filling of choice.
